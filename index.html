About

The person behind the systems.

Kibo
Sr. Manager, RevOps & MarketingOps

I've spent my career asking one question: what does it actually take to make revenue predictable?

I'm Sajan. I started in marketing — segmentation, email, campaigns. Then I got curious about why some campaigns converted and others didn't. That curiosity pulled me deeper into data, then into systems, then into the full revenue engine.

At Kibo, I built the GTM machine from scratch. When I joined, we were at $6M ARR. Two years later, we crossed $43M. That wasn't magic — it was careful plumbing: intent signal monitoring, a proper LTV calculator, a 3M+ contact database kept clean, and 30+ dashboards that let every team answer their own questions.

I'm finishing a PhD in Marketing Analytics at SRM University. When I'm not building systems, I'm usually asking why a number moved — and not stopping until I have a real answer.
